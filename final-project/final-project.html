Personal Training

If you are personal training on a regular basis, you and your trainer will discuss your goals and reassess them periodically. Sessions will consist of full body strength and conditioning workouts that will be varied to maximize results. During each workout, your trainer will ensure that you are lifting safely and using correct technique. In addition, he/she will provide cardio workouts to be done on your own and nutritional guidance. If weight loss is your goal, your trainer will monitor your progress with weigh-ins and measurements.

Family Owned & Operated

Joshua and Anna May opened CINCO fit in May of 2012. Prior to opening CINCO fit, both were full time high school science teachers and cross country and track coaches. After having their son Miles, they then decided to pursue their lifelong dream of owning a gym. At CINCO fit, Anna and Joshua hope to pass on our passion for fitness and running to the community. They also aspire to help as many Katy residents reach their fitness goals, whether that means simply providing a facility for them, finding groups for them to train with, or helping them with individualized workout plans.
